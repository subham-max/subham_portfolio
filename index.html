NTPC Sanjeevani 2.0

I developed a comprehensive Hospital Management System for NTPC using Angular 17, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Java, designed to streamline the healthcare process for employees and ex-employees. The system integrates biometric authentication through fingerprint and face verification, allowing users to securely enroll in hospitals. With special quotas for government employees, the platform efficiently manages all hospital-related records, from admission to discharge. It tracks the entire patient journey, ensuring that NTPC employees receive the appropriate care and benefits. This project enhances administrative efficiency and provides a seamless experience for both hospitals and employees by automating key processes and maintaining accurate healthcare records.

This project enhances transparency and efficiency in land management, offering an intuitive interface for government officials to access and update land-related information in real-time.
